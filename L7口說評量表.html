A

Mostly confident. Maintains eye contact most of the time. Pace and volume mostly appropriate. Clear structure with a recognizable opening, body, and closing. Slight weakness in one area. B
 Idea is present but somewhat vague. Message could be clearer or more focused. Some vocabulary and grammar errors. Communication is understandable but limited. Somewhat nervous. C

Difficult to follow at times. Structure is weak. Difficult to identify a clear opening or closing. Needs improvement — partially meets expectations. Significant revision needed.
