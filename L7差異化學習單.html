Part 1 · Critical Thinking 批判思考
QUESTION 1 · Agree or Disagree
Do you agree that 18 minutes is the ideal length for a talk? Use evidence from the passage and your own experience to support your opinion.
QUESTION 2 · Evaluation
TED's motto is "Ideas Worth Spreading." What makes an idea "worth spreading"? Give two criteria and explain your reasoning.
QUESTION 3 · Comparison
How is watching a TED Talk different from reading a textbook? Which do you find more effective for learning, and why? Use at least one vocabulary word from this lesson.
QUESTION 4 · Application
Rewrite the following sentence using the keep / leave / find pattern. Write at least two different versions.

"Watching TED Talks makes me feel motivated and makes me want to learn more."
